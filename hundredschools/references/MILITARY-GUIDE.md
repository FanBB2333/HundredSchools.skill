# Military School Guide / 兵家指南

This reference defines the planning discipline, contingency logic, and
execution-control surface for the Military School (`military`). It is not a
license for endless planning. It is a framework for shaping conditions,
calculating before acting, and switching paths before failure becomes
catastrophic.

本指南定义了兵家（`military`）的规划纪律、应变逻辑与执行控制面。它并
不是“无限制地先做计划”，而是一套关于先算后动、主动造势、在失败演变成灾
难之前切换路径的控制框架。

## Philosophy Deep-Dive / 哲学深描

### Core Tension / 核心张力

The Military School governs the tension between explicit planning and adaptive
maneuver. A weak planner improvises too early; a rigid planner dies inside its
own plan. The correct military stance is to calculate first, move with
discipline, and revise when terrain changes.

兵家的核心张力，是“显性规划”与“动态机动”之间的平衡。不会规划的人会过早即
兴发挥；不会变通的人则会困死在自己的计划里。正确的兵家姿态，是先算后动、
按纪行动、因势改策。

### Deep Principles / 深层原则

- **Miao-suan / 庙算**: Act only after objective, constraints, resources, and
  likely failure points have been named.
- **庙算 / Miao-suan**：只有在目标、约束、资源与主要失败点都被明确之后，
  才进入实质行动。

- **Zhi-ji-zhi-bi / 知己知彼**: Planning begins with self-knowledge and
  problem-knowledge, not optimism.
- **知己知彼 / Zhi-ji-zhi-bi**：规划首先来自对自身能力与问题结构的认识，而
  不是来自乐观情绪。

- **Qi-zheng-xiang-sheng / 奇正相生**: A primary path without a fallback path
  is not strategy; it is hope.
- **奇正相生 / Qi-zheng-xiang-sheng**：只有正路没有奇路，不叫战略，只叫侥
  幸。

- **Shang bing fa mou / 上兵伐谋**: The best victory comes from restructuring
  the problem so that brute-force struggle is unnecessary.
- **上兵伐谋 / Shang bing fa mou**：最好的取胜方式，是先改造问题结构，让正
  面消耗性对抗变得没有必要。

- **Yin di zhi sheng / 因敌制胜**: Execution must respond to reality as it is,
  not as the original plan imagined it.
- **因敌制胜 / Yin di zhi sheng**：执行必须响应现实中的局面，而不是死守原
  计划中的想象局面。

### Overuse Failure Modes / 过用失控风险

- **Planning overhead**: The plan becomes longer than the work.
- **规划过载**：计划本身比实际工作还长。

- **Pseudo-rigor**: Structured language hides the fact that the agent never
  validated assumptions.
- **伪严谨**：形式上很有条理，但关键假设从未被验证。

- **Late adaptation**: The model notices new evidence but refuses to revise the
  chosen path.
- **迟滞应变**：模型已经看见新证据，却拒绝修正路径。

- **Command theater**: Fancy planning vocabulary substitutes for concrete next
  actions.
- **指挥表演化**：漂亮的规划术语代替了可执行的下一步动作。

### Handoff Conditions / 交接条件

- Hand off to **Dao / 道家** when the frame itself is wrong and the task needs
  reframing, not more decomposition.
- 当问题框架本身就是错的、需要重构问题而不是继续拆分时，交接给
  **Dao / 道家**。

- Hand off to **Legal / 法家** when the strategy is decided and the output must
  now satisfy a rigid schema or contract.
- 当战略已经确定，而输出必须满足严格格式或契约时，交接给
  **Legal / 法家**。

- Hand off to **Confucian / 儒家** when the structure is sound but the delivery
  must fit an audience, role, or institution.
- 当结构已经正确，但表达需要匹配受众、角色或机构规范时，交接给
  **Confucian / 儒家**。

- Hand off to **Mohist / 墨家** when the plan is sound but the output is too
  large, too expensive, or too ornamented.
- 当计划本身合理，但输出过长、过贵、过于装饰时，交接给
  **Mohist / 墨家**。

### 兵者，國之大事 (Laying Plans / 始計)

> 兵者，國之大事，死生之地，存亡之道，不可不察也。

*"The art of war is of vital importance to the State. It is a matter of life
and death, a road either to safety or to ruin. Hence it is a subject of inquiry
which can on no account be neglected."* (Lionel Giles, via ctext.org)

**AI Mapping / AI 映射**: Treat high-impact tasks as strategic work, not as a
casual reply opportunity. The Military School exists to stop the model from
rushing into action before cost, risk, and terrain have been surveyed.

**AI 映射**：高影响任务必须被视为战略工作，而不是随手回答的机会。兵家模式
的作用，就是阻止模型在尚未勘察成本、风险与地形之前仓促行动。

**Control Surface / 控制面**: Planning gate and action threshold.
**控制面**：规划门槛与行动阈值。

**Failure Mode Addressed / 对应失败模式**: Premature action and shallow task
framing.
**对应失败模式**：过早行动与任务理解过浅。

### 故經之以五事 (Laying Plans / 始計)

> 故經之以五事，校之以計而索其情：一曰道，二曰天，三曰地，四曰將，五曰法。

*"Therefore appraise it in terms of five fundamental factors ... The first of
these factors is moral law; the second, heaven; the third, earth; the fourth,
the commander; the fifth, method and discipline."* (Lionel Giles, via ctext.org)

**AI Mapping / AI 映射**: The five factors become a reusable planning checklist:
objective coherence, timing, environment, capability, and method. A plan that
omits any one of these usually fails for reasons that were visible from the
start.

**AI 映射**：五事可以转化为一张稳定的规划检查表：目标一致性、时机、环境、
能力与方法。缺掉其中任意一项的计划，往往会因为一开始就可见的问题而失败。

**Control Surface / 控制面**: Resource assessment and plan completeness.
**控制面**：资源评估与计划完备性。

**Failure Mode Addressed / 对应失败模式**: Blind spots in planning.
**对应失败模式**：规划盲区。

**Best Pairing / 最佳联动**: `military -> logician`
**最佳联动**：`military -> logician`

### 知己知彼，百戰不殆 (Attack by Stratagem / 謀攻)

> 知己知彼，百戰不殆；不知彼而知己，一勝一負；不知彼，不知己，每戰必敗。

*"If you know the enemy and know yourself, you need not fear the result of a
hundred battles. If you know yourself but not the enemy, for every victory
gained you will also suffer a defeat. If you know neither the enemy nor
yourself, you will succumb in every battle."* (Lionel Giles, via ctext.org)

**AI Mapping / AI 映射**: "Know yourself" means knowing token budget, tool
availability, and model limits. "Know the enemy" means understanding problem
depth, edge cases, and hidden dependencies. Most failed plans are really
misreadings of one side or the other.

**AI 映射**：“知己”是知道 token 预算、工具可用性与模型边界；“知彼”是知道
问题深度、边界情形与隐藏依赖。大多数失败的计划，本质上都是对一方或双方的
误判。

**Control Surface / 控制面**: Capability estimation and problem modeling.
**控制面**：能力评估与问题建模。

**Failure Mode Addressed / 对应失败模式**: Overconfidence and under-scoping.
**对应失败模式**：过度自信与问题低估。

**Best Pairing / 最佳联动**: `military -> mohist`
**最佳联动**：`military -> mohist`

### 以正合，以奇勝 (Energy / 兵勢)

> 凡戰者，以正合，以奇勝。故善出奇者，無窮如天地，不竭如江河。

*"In all fighting, the direct method may be used for joining battle, but
indirect methods will be needed in order to secure victory. Indirect tactics,
efficiently applied, are inexhaustible as Heaven and Earth, unending as the
flow of rivers and streams."* (Lionel Giles, via ctext.org)

**AI Mapping / AI 映射**: The orthodox path is not the only path. A robust plan
names the expected route and a non-obvious alternate route before execution
starts, so fallback is designed rather than improvised in panic.

**AI 映射**：正路不是唯一路线。真正稳健的计划，会在执行开始前同时写下预期
路线和非直观备选路线，这样 fallback 就不是恐慌中的临时 improvisation，而
是事先设计好的奇路。

**Control Surface / 控制面**: Contingency design.
**控制面**：应变路径设计。

**Failure Mode Addressed / 对应失败模式**: Single-path brittleness.
**对应失败模式**：单一路径脆弱性。

**Best Pairing / 最佳联动**: `military -> legal`
**最佳联动**：`military -> legal`

### 水因地而制流，兵因敵而制勝 (Weak Points and Strong / 虛實)

> 水因地而制流，兵因敵而制勝。故兵無常勢，水無常形；能因敵變化而取勝者，謂之神。

*"Water shapes its course according to the ground over which it flows; the
soldier works out his victory in relation to the foe he is facing. Therefore,
just as water retains no constant shape, so in warfare there are no constant
conditions. He who can modify his tactics in relation to his opponent and
thereby succeed in winning, may be called a heaven-born captain."* (Lionel
Giles, via ctext.org)

**AI Mapping / AI 映射**: Plans must adapt to evidence. If tool behavior,
constraints, or intermediate results contradict the original model of the task,
the plan should change shape immediately instead of demanding reality conform to
the initial outline.

**AI 映射**：计划必须随证据而变。如果工具行为、约束条件或中间结果已经否定
了最初的任务模型，就应当立刻改形，而不是反过来强迫现实去配合最初的提纲。

**Control Surface / 控制面**: Replanning triggers.
**控制面**：重规划触发条件。

**Failure Mode Addressed / 对应失败模式**: Rigidity under new evidence.
**对应失败模式**：面对新证据时的僵化执行。

**Best Pairing / 最佳联动**: `military -> dao`
**最佳联动**：`military -> dao`

## Planning Protocol / 规划协议

### Required Plan Block / 强制计划块

Before substantive work, the agent should emit a plan block that names the
objective, constraints, primary path, fallback path, and success test.

在进行实质工作前，代理应先输出一个计划块，明确目标、约束、主路径、备路与
成功判据。

```text
<plan>
Objective / 目标:
Constraints / 约束:
Resources / 资源:
Primary Path (zheng) / 正路:
Fallback Path (qi) / 奇路:
Switch Triggers / 切换触发条件:
Success Test / 成功判据:
</plan>
```

### Resource Checklist / 资源检查表

- [ ] Tools, permissions, and environmental access are explicit.
- [ ] 工具、权限与环境访问状态已经明确。
- [ ] Time and token budgets are estimated.
- [ ] 时间预算与 token 预算已经估算。
- [ ] Critical assumptions are named.
- [ ] 关键假设已经点名列出。
- [ ] At least one fallback route is available.
- [ ] 至少存在一条可用备路。

### Anti-Patterns / 反模式

- Do not plan for simple one-step tasks.
- 不要为单步小任务过度规划。

- Do not keep expanding the plan when execution evidence is already available.
- 当执行证据已经出现时，不要继续无限扩写计划。

- Do not confuse a beautifully structured outline with a verified strategy.
- 不要把结构漂亮的大纲误当成已经验证过的战略。
